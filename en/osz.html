It is an online shopping mall that sells a wide range of categories, including cosmetics, food, household goods, and health products, with a focus on cosmetics. This site was created for portfolio purposes, and instead of a unique design, it was centered around layouts, banners, and product detail pages that are currently applicable and commonly used in online shopping malls.

This site was not designed by simply selecting standout images from various design sites. All product thumbnails and banners were designed in-house, and random images were used to reflect potential issues that could arise in real-world scenarios, ensuring that the design process and planning took into account possible design challenges.
